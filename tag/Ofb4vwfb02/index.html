Standing Cushion
2019-07-16 2 min read # IoT # Tangible Interaction # TAM # HCI # Interaction Design

[July 2018 - July 2019]
Master Thesis

"How to develop a Device to remind Elderly people do more activities when they sitting for long time in home?" is the research question. This thesis is meant to address these problems by analysing the required infrastructure of an Internet of Things in home, which was developed following concepts of user-centered design and usability. After the analysis, presenting a new assistive system based on Embed Interaction tech for enabling low-cost sensing on everyday objects.
